what is doer ideation challenge?


The Doer Ideation Challenge is a comprehensive process of idea identification and the recognition of potential entrepreneurs within the NERIST campus. It aims to educate, empower, and support students in problem identification, gaining insights about the problem, and developing unique solutions.

Doer will reward the top three ideas with exciting prizes and also recognize everyone with a certificate as a physical evidence of their entrepreneurial and problem solving abilities

Online registration opens: July 19, 2023

Participants can register online through the Doer Ideation Challenge website. The registration form will ask for basic information such as name, email address, and contact details.

Last day to register: August 31, 2023

On August 31, 2023, participants will need to ensure that they have completed their online registration for the Doer Ideation Challenge. It is important to note that this will be the final day to register, and no registrations will be accepted after this deadline.

Ideation challenge: September 4, 2023

The ideation challenge will be at NERIST campus participants will have the opportunity to present their ideas to a panel of judges. The judges will be looking for ideas that are creative, innovative, and have the potential to make a difference in the world.

Shortlist Announcement: September 15, 2023

The shortlisted teams will be notified by email. The shortlisted teams will have the opportunity to access the Doer Smart Incubation Program..

Doer Smart Incubation starts: October 1, 2023

The Doer Smart Incubation Program is a 6-month program that provides participants with the resources and mentorship they need to turn their ideas into investment ready. The program includes workshops, training, and access to a network of industry experts.
